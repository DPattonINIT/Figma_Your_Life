Inspiration


Lamar has been inspired by many throughout the years. Chef Anthony Bourdain has single handily made the biggest impact in his career as a Chef and food reviewer.

Anthony Bourdain was a groundbreaking chef, author, and television personality whose adventurous spirit and candid storytelling transformed the way we view food and travel. With his hit shows like "No Reservations" and "Parts Unknown," Bourdain traveled to far-flung corners of the globe, immersing himself in local cultures and cuisines while exploring deeper social issues.
